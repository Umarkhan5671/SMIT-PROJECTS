JUNOON

The love is music

Junoon directed by founder, lead guitarist and songwriter, Salman Ahmad, who was soon joined by keyboardist Nusrat Hussain, bass guitarist Brian O'Connell and vocalist Ali Azmat.Junoon is Pakistan's and one of South Asia's most successful bands.Since their inception, the group has released a total of nineteen albums: seven studio albums; one soundtrack; two live albums; four video albums; and five compilations.They have sold over 30 million records worldwide.
